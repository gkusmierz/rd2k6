RDAdmin - Audio Ports Help

Mode - short for Channel Mode, configures the Left and Right behaviour when recording.

Behaviour varies depending on the number of channels to record as summarized in the table below:

ChannelsModeEffect
MonoNormalL+R sum to mono
MonoSwapR+L sum to mono (same result as Normal)
MonoLeft onlyL -> mono
MonoRight onlyR -> mono
StereoNormalStereo
StereoSwapSwapped stereo
StereoLeft onlyL -> to L channel only, R channel is silent
StereoRight onlyR -> to R channel only, L channel is silent
