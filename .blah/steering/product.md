# Product Overview

Photo Albums is a web application for managing, browsing, and sharing personal photo collections. It serves individual users who need an intuitive way to organize photos into albums, tag them for easy retrieval, and share content with other users.

## Core Capabilities

- **Album Management**: Create, edit, delete, and browse photo albums
- **Photo Upload and Processing**: Upload images (JPEG, PNG, WebP) with automatic thumbnail generation
- **Tagging and Search**: Categorize photos with tags and search/filter across the entire library
- **Sharing**: Grant read-only access to albums or individual photos with other users
- **Photo Browsing**: Grid-based thumbnail views with full-size detail navigation

## Target Use Cases

- Organizing personal photos into themed or event-based albums
- Quickly finding specific photos using tag-based search
- Sharing photo collections with friends or family in a controlled, read-only manner

## Value Proposition

A focused, privacy-respecting photo management tool where users retain full control over their content. Sharing is explicit and revocable, albums are personal by default, and access is always scoped to owned or granted resources.

## Scope Boundaries

- Web-only (no mobile-native apps in current iteration)
- No social features beyond direct sharing (no comments, likes, or public galleries)
- No video support or advanced image editing
- Shared albums are read-only (no collaborative editing)
